# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 12, 2026_

MiraLingo is a small language-learning web app for practicing Mirad vocabulary, phrases, pronunciation, and review. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and the choices you have.

## Information we collect

When you create or use an account, MiraLingo may collect:

- **Account information**: email address, optional display name or nickname, password hash, authentication sessions, and account settings.
- **Practice activity**: practice cards shown to you, answers you submit, whether answers were correct, review state, progress history, streaks, and achievement events.
- **Learning preferences**: theme, text-to-speech speed, autoplay setting, and sound-effect settings.
- **Admin and support metadata**: account creation time, last activity time, and account status used to operate the service and support account deletion.
- **Technical information**: basic server logs such as request path, response status, timestamps, and error diagnostics needed to keep the app reliable.

## How we use information

MiraLingo uses collected information to:

- create and secure your account;
- keep you signed in;
- deliver practice cards and adapt review timing;
- track progress, accuracy, streaks, and achievements;
- save your settings across sessions;
- provide password reset and account deletion features;
- diagnose errors, prevent abuse, and maintain the web app.

## What we do not do

MiraLingo does not sell your personal information. MiraLingo does not use your practice answers for advertising. Passwords are not stored in plain text.

## Cookies and local storage

MiraLingo uses authentication cookies or session storage to keep you signed in. The frontend may also use browser storage for session state and preferences. You can clear this data in your browser, but doing so may sign you out or reset local preferences.

## Third-party services

MiraLingo may use third-party services for login, hosting, email delivery, or infrastructure. For example, Google sign-in may be available if configured. These services process information according to their own policies.

## Data retention

Account and practice data are kept while your account exists so MiraLingo can show your progress and continue review scheduling. If you delete your account, the app deletes account-linked learning data according to the deletion feature's current behavior, except for any data that must be retained for security, debugging, backups, or legal reasons.

## Your choices

You can:

- choose not to create an account;
- update learning settings in the app;
- request a password reset;
- delete your account from Settings when available;
- contact the project maintainer if you need help with account data.

## Security

MiraLingo uses reasonable safeguards for a small educational web app, including hashed passwords and authenticated account actions. No system is perfectly secure, so use a unique password and avoid sharing sensitive personal information in practice answers.

## Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as MiraLingo changes. The latest version will be shown in the web app.

## Contact

For questions about this policy or your data, contact the project maintainer through the project repository or support channel used for MiraLingo.
